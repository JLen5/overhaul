Frequently Asked Questions
> What is InkLink?
INKLINK is an innovative note-taking app designed to enhance accessibility for various user groups, including English language learners and individuals with disabilities. It offers unique features catering to diverse needs in note-taking.
> What sets INKLINK apart from other note-taking apps?
INKLINK distinguishes itself by prioritizing accessibility and user-friendliness. Its features include speech-to-text functions, screen readers, language translations, text-to-speech capabilities, and captioning, making it a versatile tool for a wide range of users.
> Who is INKLINK designed for?
INKLINK primarily serves two main groups: students with language barriers and those who struggle with traditional note-taking due to disabilities. However, its inclusive design welcomes other potential user demographics that align with the app's core values of accessibility.
